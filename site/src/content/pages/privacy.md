Short version: the only personal information Jigg.AI collects is your email address, and only if you choose to subscribe. It's used to send you build logs — nothing else — and it's never sold or shared.

## What's collected

- If you subscribe: your email address (and your name, if you give it). That's it.
- If you don't subscribe: nothing you actively hand over.

## How it's used

Your email is stored with [EMAIL PROVIDER] and used only to send one email per new build. You can unsubscribe from any email at any time, using the link at the bottom of it — and your address is removed.

## What's never done

Your data is never sold, rented, or handed to advertisers. There's no third-party ad tracking on this site.

## Technical basics

The site is hosted on Netlify and served through Cloudflare. Like any website, those providers process standard technical data — such as IP addresses and request logs — to deliver the site and keep it secure. That's ordinary infrastructure, not something Jigg.AI mines.
