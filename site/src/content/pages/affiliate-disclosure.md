Some of the links to tools on this site are affiliate links. If you sign up or buy through one, Jigg.AI may earn a commission — at no extra cost to you.

Here's what that changes about the write-ups: nothing.

## The rules I hold myself to

- Tools are chosen on merit — worth your time, accessible, and actually useful for building something real. An affiliate program never buys coverage.
- Every build is tested and written up honestly, including what broke and what didn't work. The affiliate relationship doesn't soften the verdict.
- Affiliate links are always disclosed, and always placed *after* the proof — once you've seen what was actually built — never before it.
- If a tool isn't worth it, the post says so, affiliate program or not.

The commissions are what let Jigg.AI keep building in the open. But the whole project only works if you can trust the write-ups — so the proof always comes first, and the link always comes second.
