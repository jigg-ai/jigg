# Subscribe — one honest build in your inbox

Source page: https://jigg.ai/subscribe/

Every new build log, sent once — with the prompts, the real costs, the test results, and
the reproducible files. One email per build. No filler, no drip sequence.

## How to subscribe

Enter your email address in the signup form on the Subscribe page
(https://jigg.ai/subscribe/) or in the signup box on the home page, then submit it. The
list is run with **Buttondown**, the newsletter service that sends the emails.

- You get **one email per new build** — nothing else.
- You can **unsubscribe at any time** using the link at the bottom of any email, and your
  address is removed.
- Your email address is never sold or shared. See https://jigg.ai/privacy/.

## Navigating the site

The top navigation is: **Builds · Tools · About · Subscribe**.

- **Builds** (https://jigg.ai/builds/) — every build log, newest first, with category
  filters and verified dates.
- **Tools** (https://jigg.ai/tools/) — the tools actually tested, with a plain verdict
  and an accessibility read. No star ratings.
- **About** (https://jigg.ai/about/) — what Jigg.AI is and who's behind it.
- **Subscribe** (https://jigg.ai/subscribe/) — the email list.

The footer also links Privacy and Affiliate disclosure. Questions: hello@jigg.ai.
